Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how the Zedmonkey extension handles your data.

Data Collection

The Zedmonkey extension does not collect personal data from users. It operates locally within your browser and does not transmit data to external servers.

Permissions

The extension requires certain permissions to function effectively:

  • Storage: Used to save scripts and settings locally within your browser.
  • Scripting: Allows the extension to execute scripts on web pages as per user configuration.
  • ContextMenus: Enables the extension to add options to the browser's context menu for ease of use.
  • Host Permissions: The extension can access all URLs to apply scripts as configured by the user.

Third-Party Services

The extension may interact with third-party services for script synchronization, such as Dropbox, OneDrive, Google Drive, or WebDAV. These interactions are initiated by the user and are subject to the privacy policies of the respective services.

Security

We prioritize the security of your data. The extension is designed to operate securely within your browser environment, and we recommend keeping your browser and extension updated to the latest versions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review the policy periodically.

For any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at livrasand@outlook.com.
